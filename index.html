Basic plan overview

pNous

We have created a new system that changes the way we look at humans. pNous is a project that plans to improve the lives of people by releasing applications and building hubs where people can improve the way they take in information and make decisions.

Background

We have done extensive research on the way we look at intelligence and the works of many psychologists. After almost a year and a half of research we noticed patterns that emerged during our research and created a system that defines a human. We conducted experiments with a controlled group of students and found our data to be consistent and correlate with the respected fields we tested. Then came the idea that we can use this new system to help improve the lives of countless people.

The Information Part

The first step we would like to take is to improve the average students grades. Students are the future and it is our intend to empower students by teaching them how they best take in information and can improve on their weaker way of taking it in. We also want to show educators how to present the information in a way that all students can understand. The way we will be doing this is by releasing applications for both students and educators.
